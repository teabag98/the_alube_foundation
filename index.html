MISSION

To address and treat mental health conditions before they cause individual suffering

VISION

To be the leading solutions provider of mental health in Kenya

GOALS

Provide preventive measures on mental illnesses Early identification of mental related illnesses and intervention of symptoms

PROGRAMS AND ADVOCACY WORK

Alube foundation seeks to conduct workshops in schools, workplaces and publicsocial amenities throughout communities with the aim of developing policy that can be legislated to become law in the near future.

ACCESS TO HELP

ALUBE foundation offers information on how to access help for yourself and your loved ones, including useful information on available treatment and support as well as tools for recovery and support.
